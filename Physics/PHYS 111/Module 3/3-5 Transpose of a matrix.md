Essentially just interchanging the rows and columns of a matrix.

For example, given
$$
B =
	\begin{bmatrix}
		5 & -2 \\
		1 & 3
	\end{bmatrix}
$$
then
$$
B^T =
	\begin{bmatrix}
		5 & 1 \\
		-2 & 3
	\end{bmatrix}
$$

#### General relationship between transposition and dimensions
If $A$ has the dimensions $m \times n$, then $A^T$ has dimensions $n \times m$.
