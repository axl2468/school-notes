Condition:
Both matrices have to be the same dimension.

Addition is simple: just add the respective elements in each column-row position in both matrices.

#### Example:
Given matrices $B$ and $C$:
$$
\begin{align}
B &=
	\begin{bmatrix}
		5 & -2 \\
		1 & 3
	\end{bmatrix}
\\
C &=
	\begin{bmatrix}
		3 & 1 \\
		-2 & 0
	\end{bmatrix}
\\\\
B+C &= 
	\begin{bmatrix}
		5+3 & -2+1 \\
		1-2 & 3+0
	\end{bmatrix}
\\
&=
	\begin{bmatrix}
		8 & -1 \\
		-1 & 3
	\end{bmatrix}
\end{align}
$$
